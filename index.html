About Sanskriti College of Science & Tech

Sanskriti College of Science & Technology stands as a symbol of academic excellence and modern innovation. With an eco-friendly, serene campus and futuristic infrastructure, the college fosters intellectual growth, practical learning, and holistic development.

Our Mission

To nurture competent, ethical, and globally aware professionals by delivering quality education combined with real-world experience and moral guidance.

Vision

To emerge as a leading center of academic excellence and research, shaping minds to lead with knowledge, compassion, and a futuristic mindset.

Campus Life

A vibrant atmosphere where students thrive through tech fests, cultural programs, innovation clubs, and sports—blending learning with joy and self-growth.

Facilities

  • Smart Classrooms: Equipped with projectors, audio-visual aids, and interactive boards.
  • Modern Laboratories: High-end labs for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Computer Science, and AI/ML.
  • Central Library: Vast collection of books, e-journals, research papers, and a digital reading hall.
  • Wi-Fi Enabled Campus: 24/7 high-speed internet access in academic blocks and hostels.
  • Separate Hostels: Safe, secure, and hygienic hostels for boys and girls with mess and laundry services.
  • Auditorium & Seminar Halls: Fully air-conditioned halls for workshops, cultural events, and guest lectures.
  • Cafeteria: Multi-cuisine cafeteria offering healthy, hygienic, and affordable meals.
  • Medical Facility: In-house health center with regular doctor visits and emergency care.
  • Sports & Recreation: Indoor gym, yoga studio, outdoor stadium, and courts for cricket, basketball, badminton, etc.
  • Transportation: College buses covering key areas for safe and comfortable commuting.
  • 24x7 Security: CCTV surveillance and security guards ensuring student safety at all times.
  • Incubation Center: Facilities and mentorship for student startups and innovations.
  • Tech Clubs & Innovation Labs: Dedicated spaces for coding, robotics, IoT, and entrepreneurship.

Faculty Excellence

Our faculty are researchers, innovators, and mentors, dedicated to transforming classrooms into launchpads of talent, innovation, and leadership.

Industry Tie-ups

Strong collaborations with top MNCs and startups ensure hands-on learning through internships, seminars, workshops, and placement training programs.
